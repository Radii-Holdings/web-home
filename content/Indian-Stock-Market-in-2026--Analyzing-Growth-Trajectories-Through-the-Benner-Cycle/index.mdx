History doesn't repeat itself, but it often rhymes.
In 1875, Samuel Benner, a prosperous farmer wiped out by the 1873 panic, discovered a cycle in corn and pig iron prices that has uncannily predicted stock market crashes for 150 years.

## Decoding the Cycle 🔄

The Benner Cycle operates on a 16-18-20 year rhythm.
*   **2023-2026 (Phase B):** "Years of Good Times." We are currently in the final leg of this phase.
*   **2027 (Phase C):** "Years of Hard Times." A cyclical liquidity crunch or recession.
