## Top Blue-chip Stocks for a Stable Portfolio

### Introduction

Blue-chip stocks are known for their stability, reliability, and consistent returns. This blog explores the top blue-chip stocks in the Indian market that can help build a stable investment portfolio.

![Blue-chip Stocks](../../public/blogs/anne-nygard-vDwGiU4uKYU-unsplash.jpg)

### What are Blue-chip Stocks?

#### Definition

Blue-chip stocks are shares of large, well-established, and financially sound companies with a history of reliable performance.

#### Importance

1. **Stability**: Less volatile compared to smaller companies.
2. **Dividend Payments**: Regular dividends provide steady income.

### Top Blue-chip Stocks

#### Reliance Industries

- **Overview**: Largest conglomerate in India with diversified business interests.
- **Technical Analysis**: Strong upward trend with support at ₹2,000.
- **Fundamental Analysis**: Refer to [Reliance Annual Report](https://www.ril.com/InvestorRelations/FinancialReporting.aspx).

#### HDFC Bank

- **Overview**: Leading private sector bank with robust financial health.
- **Technical Analysis**: Consistent growth with key support at ₹1,500.
- **Fundamental Analysis**: See [HDFC Bank Annual Report](https://www.hdfcbank.com/personal/about-us/investor-relations/annual-reports).

#### Tata Consultancy Services (TCS)

- **Overview**: Major player in the IT services sector.
- **Technical Analysis**: Bullish momentum with resistance at ₹3,500.
- **Fundamental Analysis**: Check [TCS Financials](https://www.tcs.com/investor-relations).

#### Infosys

- **Overview**: A leading IT services company known for its strong financial performance.
- **Technical Analysis**: Steady growth with support at ₹1,600.
- **Fundamental Analysis**: Refer to [Infosys Annual Report](https://www.infosys.com/investors/reports-filings/annual-report.html).

#### ITC Limited

- **Overview**: Diversified conglomerate with interests in FMCG, hotels, and more.
- **Technical Analysis**: Upward trend with key support at ₹250.
- **Fundamental Analysis**: See [ITC Financials](https://www.itcportal.com/investor/investor-relations.aspx).
